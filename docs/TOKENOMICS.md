# BTCLE Tokenomics

## Core Supply Model

- Name: Bitcoin Limited Edition
- Symbol: BTCLE
- Max Supply: `210,000 BTCLE`
- Model: Fixed-supply, non-inflationary

## Distribution Summary

- Circulating at launch: `21,000 BTCLE` (10%)
- Locked and vested allocation: `189,000 BTCLE` (90%)
- Vesting period: 10 years
- Vesting timeline: March 2026 to December 2035
- Vesting reference: UNCX Network lock/vesting setup

## Liquidity and Release Approach

The project describes a gradual monthly unlock model with declining emissions over time, designed to reduce short-term sell pressure and support long-term market stability.

The documentation also states that a large portion of unlocked tokens is allocated to liquidity on PancakeSwap V3 with lock transparency.

## Governance and Utility

BTCLE utility is documented as:

- governance participation
- ecosystem incentives
- access to future Layer-2 ecosystem features
